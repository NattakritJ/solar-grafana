<objective>
Integrate CT meters into the dashboard: replace House Load panels with CT-direct measurement, add a new Power Distribution row with per-floor power and reconciliation stats, and add Floor 1/2 series to the Power Profile time-series chart.

The new Power Distribution section lets the homeowner see per-floor power distribution at a glance, and the reconciliation panels show how closely CT measurement matches the derived calculation.

- Panels 5 and 10 using CT Floor1+Floor2 queries (replacing East/West/Grid derived formula)
