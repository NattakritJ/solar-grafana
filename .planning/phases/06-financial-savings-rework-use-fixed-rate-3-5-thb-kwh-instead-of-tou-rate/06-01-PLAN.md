<objective>
Replace the TOU (time-of-use) rate savings calculation with a single flat rate of 3.5 THB/kWh across all three savings panels (Today, Month, Year), and remove the four TOU-specific breakdown panels (Peak kWh, Peak THB, Off-Peak kWh, Off-Peak THB) which become meaningless with a flat rate.
