<objective>
Migrate 8 solar AC power panels from slow-polling inverter tables (`"East Microinverter"` / `"West Microinverter"`) to fast CT sensor tables (`east_microinverter_power` / `west_microinverter_power`), tighten recency windows on all 15 CT-table snapshot panels from 1 minute to 10 seconds, and increase dashboard refresh from 10s to 5s.

Purpose: The inverter measurements log at ~60s intervals with stale/repeated values. The new CT sensors clamp directly on the inverter AC output cables and log at ~2s intervals, providing live, accurate power readings. Tightening the recency window and refresh interval takes full advantage of this faster data rate.

Output: `solar-pv-monitor.json` with surgical SQL replacements covering 8 migrated panels + 15 CT window tightenings + refresh interval change. No new panels, no layout changes.
</objective>
